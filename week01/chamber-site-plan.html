Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Fort Worth Chamber of Commerce aims to...

  • Promote local businesses and economic development.
  • Show Americans everywhere that this is where the West begins, and demonstrate the history that made this city.

Scenarios

  • What events are scheduled this month for historical showcasing?
  • Where can I find contact information for the board of directors?
  • What are the current economic trends and growth projections for Fort Worth?

Color Schema

Main colors:

  • Background color: Drab Dark Brown
  • Accent color for links and buttons: Ecru
  • Paragraph color: Nyanza
  • Extra Accent color: Sage

Typography
